Grilled Cheese

Follow this recipe to learn how to make a delicious Grilled Cheese, Joe Beef style!

This simple recipe will provide you with a delicious sandwich in no less than 15 minutes!

Ingredients

  • 4 Joe Beef Montreal-style seasoned bagelwiches
  • 100g Sensations by Compliments Old-Fashioned Hot Capicollo
  • 100g Emmenthal cheese
  • 125ml (Â½ cup) baby spinach

Preparation

  1. Evenly distribute the capicollo, cheese, and spinach onto four bagelwich halves. Top sandwiches with remaining halves.
  2. Cook in a skillet heated over medium-low heat or a panini press until cheese is gooey and the bread golden brown. Serve.
